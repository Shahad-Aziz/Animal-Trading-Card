Penguin

penguins are amazing birds! They are not quite like any bird you have seen before in your backyard or at the park. They have adapted to their cold environment in a way that makes them pretty special.

  • Flight: Penguins are flightless birds
  • live: Most penguins live in the Southern Hemisphere
  • Body length: The Emperor Penguin is the tallest of all penguin species, reaching as tall as 120 cm (47 in) in height.

Penguins are aquatic, flightless birds that are highly adapted to life in the water.
